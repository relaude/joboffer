
=== XLSX SHEET: 01_Epic_Overview (10x2) ===
Field | Detail
Epic / Initiative | Online Job Offer Computation, Routing, Approval, Discussion, Acceptance, and Recording
Business Objective | The organization digitizes and standardizes the end-to-end job offer process so that recruiters, approvers, Total Rewards, and business stakeholders can improve turnaround time, governance, transparency, and audit readiness.
Primary Actors | Candidate; Talent Acquisition (TA) Partner; Total Rewards (TR) Partner; Head of Human Resources and Organizational Development (HROD Head); Division Head; President; Human Resources Business Partner (HRBP); Talent Acquisition Head (TA Head); System Administrator.
Process Coverage | The process covers candidate data gathering, salary matrix selection, salary offer computation, salary band validation, three-option salary proposal routing, approval, above-band escalation, candidate discussion, negotiation, candidate decision, documentation, recording, delegation, reporting, and governance monitoring. The process also covers push notifications, reminder notifications, escalation notifications, and approval-decision notifications.
Technology Assumption | Candidate compensation information is collected through MS Forms, while the job offer computation, routing, approval, and recording process is managed through a workflow-enabled system or platform.
Multi-Entity Requirement | The Talent Acquisition (TA) team recruits for at least three (3) companies or legal entities within one conglomerate, and the system must retrieve the correct salary matrix based on the company or legal entity linked to the requisition.
Approval Design Decision | Approvers may approve any combination of the three proposed salary options, instead of being limited to approving all three or rejecting all three.
Re-Proposal Design Decision | The Talent Acquisition (TA) Partner may withdraw and re-propose salary options when all proposals are rejected by approvers or when the candidate declines all approved offers.
Success Measures | Suggested measures include job offer turnaround time, approval turnaround time, percentage of offers within salary band, number of above-band approvals, candidate acceptance rate, number of re-proposal cycles, number of delegated transactions, and audit completion rate. Additional measures include notification delivery success rate, reminder count, overdue approval count, and average response time after notification.

Job Offer Application - Business Logic Rules
Source reviewed: Online Job Offer System - User Stories V2.xlsx. These rules are derived from the detailed programmer flowchart and are intended as implementation guidance.
Rule Table
Recommended State Transitions
New -> Awaiting Candidate Submission after TA sends the MS Forms request.
Awaiting Candidate Submission -> Submitted after candidate submits required information.
Submitted -> Returned for Correction if information or documents are incomplete, inconsistent, unreadable, or corrupted.
Submitted -> Validated after TA confirms the candidate package is complete and accurate.
Validated -> Salary Matrix Selected after legal entity and active salary matrix are confirmed.
Salary Matrix Selected -> In Analysis while TA prepares proposal options.
In Analysis -> Band Validation Completed after all proposal options are classified.
Band Validation Completed -> Pending Approval after approval tasks are created.
Pending Approval -> Pending President Approval when one or more above-band proposal options require President approval.
Pending Approval or Pending President Approval -> Approved for Candidate Discussion when at least one proposal is fully approved.
Pending Approval or Pending President Approval -> All Proposals Rejected when no submitted proposal is approved.
Approved for Candidate Discussion -> Under Candidate Review during candidate discussion.
Under Candidate Review -> Accepted when candidate accepts one approved salary option.
Under Candidate Review -> Declined when candidate declines all approved salary options.
All Proposals Rejected or Declined -> Withdrawn and Re-Proposed when TA creates a new proposal version.
Accepted -> Closed after offer letter generation and final audit records are stored.
Implementation Notes
Treat approval decisions as child records of salary proposal options, not only as a transaction-level status.
Use transaction version number to group each proposal cycle and preserve historical proposal sets.
Use salary matrix effective date and approval status in the salary matrix lookup, not only company and job level.
Use notification jobs for reminder and escalation checks so overdue approvals are processed consistently.
Write audit records in the same transaction boundary as workflow updates where possible.
Apply role-based access checks before showing compensation details, approval actions, reports, delegation controls, or generated documents.
