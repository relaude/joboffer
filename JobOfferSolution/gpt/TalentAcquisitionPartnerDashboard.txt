After reviewing the complete **02_User_Stories** workflow and the **Process Flow**, the **Talent Acquisition (TA) Partner Dashboard** should be the primary landing page after login.

The dashboard should provide visibility into the entire Job Offer lifecycle:

```text
Pre-Analysis / Data Gathering
Company & Legal Entity Setup
Job Offer Analysis
Salary Band Validation
Approval Routing
President Escalation
Candidate Discussion
Negotiation & Re-Proposal
Offer Letter Generation
```

The Process Flow shows the TA Partner as the main actor throughout the process, requiring a dashboard that highlights pending actions, approvals, candidate responses, and SLA monitoring.
