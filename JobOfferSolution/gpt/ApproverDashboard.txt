After reviewing the complete **Job Offer Process Flow**, the **Approver Dashboard** should be very different from the TA Partner Dashboard.

An Approver (HROD Head, Division Head, President) does **not manage candidates**. Their primary responsibility is to:

1. Review Job Offer proposals
2. Compare salary options
3. Review salary band validations
4. Review business justifications
5. Approve / Reject / Return for Revision
6. Monitor pending approvals
7. Track SLA compliance

The Process Flow shows that approvers receive approval tasks and review:

* Multi-option Job Offer Sheet
* Salary Band Validation Results
* Compa-Ratio
* Justification Notes
* Internal Equity Data
* Previous Approval Comments
* Candidate Information
* Company / Legal Entity Details
* Above-band exceptions requiring escalation

### Key Difference from TA Dashboard

**TA Partner Dashboard**

* Manages candidates
* Creates offers
* Negotiates
* Sends forms
* Tracks pipeline

**Approver Dashboard**

* Reviews salary proposals
* Reviews justifications
* Monitors SLA
* Handles escalations
* Approves / Rejects / Returns cases

This design follows the Approval Routing, HROD Approval, Division Head Approval, and President Escalation stages defined in the Process Flow.
