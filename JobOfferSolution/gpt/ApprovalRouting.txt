After reviewing the **Approval Routing** stages (Rows 8–10) and the **Process Flow**, the Job Offer system should not use a simple approve/reject screen.

The workflow shows a **Multi-Option Job Offer Approval Workspace** where approvers review all salary proposals in a single screen and make decisions per proposal. The process includes:

1. HROD Head Approval
2. Division Head Approval
3. President Approval (Above-Band Salaries Only)
4. Multi-option comparison
5. Approval comments
6. Approval history
7. Salary Band Validation results
8. Candidate & Requisition information

The Process Flow explicitly states:

* System creates approval workflow instance.
* Approvers review proposal comparison in one approval view.
* Approvers review each salary proposal.
* Decision is recorded per proposal.
* Above-band proposals route to President Approval.
* Approval comments and timestamps are stored.
