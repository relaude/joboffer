After reviewing the **Process Flow** for **Candidate Discussion and Acceptance** (Row 13), this stage occurs **after at least one salary proposal has completed all approval levels** and the workflow status changes to:

```text
Approved for Candidate Discussion
```

The process flow indicates that the system should:

1. Display only approved salary options.
2. Hide rejected options.
3. Display candidate contact details.
4. Allow TA Partner to discuss offer options with candidate.
5. Capture discussion notes.
6. Record candidate feedback.
7. Track acceptance, rejection, or negotiation requests.
8. Allow re-proposal if candidate declines.
9. Proceed to Offer Letter Generation if candidate accepts.

### UX Recommendation

For your ASP.NET MVC / Razor + SBAdmin2 implementation, use a **tabbed interface**:

1. **Approved Offers**
2. **Discussion Notes**
3. **Candidate Feedback**
4. **Negotiation History**
5. **Acceptance Summary**
6. **Audit Trail**

This keeps the page compact while supporting the complete Candidate Discussion and Acceptance workflow described in the process flow.
