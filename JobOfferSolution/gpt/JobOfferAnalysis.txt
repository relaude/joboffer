After reviewing the **Process Flow** and the stages **Row 6 – Job Offer Analysis** and **Row 7 – Salary Band Validation**, the Job Offer Prototype should be centered around a **single Job Offer Analysis Workspace** where the TA Partner can:

1. Review validated candidate information
2. Review Company & Legal Entity
3. View Salary Matrix
4. Create 3 Salary Proposal Options
5. Perform Salary Band Validation
6. Submit for Approval

This is consistent with the workflow shown in the ProcessFlow diagram where:

* Candidate Information is validated
* Company / Legal Entity is confirmed
* Salary Matrix is loaded
* TA Partner creates 3 salary proposals
* System computes Compa-Ratio, Salary Band Position, Increase %
* Salary Band Validation occurs
* Job Offer Analysis record is generated
* Approval Routing starts

# Salary Band Validation Panel

Displayed beside the proposal grid.

```text
--------------------------------------------------------
Salary Band Validation
--------------------------------------------------------

Minimum     ₱38,000
Midpoint    ₱45,000
Maximum     ₱52,000

--------------------------------------------------------

Option 1    WITHIN BAND     ✓
Option 2    WITHIN BAND     ✓
Option 3    WITHIN BAND     ✓

--------------------------------------------------------
```

If salary exceeds maximum:

```text
Option 3

Proposed Salary : ₱60,000

Status : ABOVE BAND

⚠ Additional President Approval Required
```

This follows the workflow where Above-Band proposals are routed to President Approval.
