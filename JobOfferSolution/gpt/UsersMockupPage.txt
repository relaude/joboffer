For a **Hybrid RBAC + ABAC** implementation, the **Job Offer Users Administration Page** becomes one of the most important administration screens because it manages:

* Azure AD linked users
* ASP.NET Identity roles
* Company access
* Legal Entity access
* Department access
* Approval limits
* ABAC attributes
* Approval routing eligibility

---

### Additional Recommendation

Create a separate **ABAC Policy Management Page**:

```text
Administration
 ├─ Job Offer Users
 ├─ Roles
 ├─ Permissions
 ├─ ABAC Policies
 ├─ Approval Matrix
 ├─ Company Setup
 └─ Legal Entity Setup
```

Then policies such as:

```text
Role = HROD Head
AND Company = User.Company
AND Salary <= User.ApprovalLimit
```

can be configured without code changes, making your Job Offer system flexible as approval rules evolve.
